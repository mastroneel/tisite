About Our Firm

Turing Investments LLC is a quantitative asset management firm that develops and deploys systematic investment strategies in futures and equities. We trade on exchanges in all major market centers worldwide using an automated system developed entirely in-house.


Founded in Palo Alto, California in 2016, our management team brings a multi-disciplinary background that includes more than a decade of experience at Goldman Sachs Asset Management and Millennium Partners LP, along with engineering positions at Silicon Valley startups.


We pursue a disciplined investment methodology with strong theoretical grounding, guided by practical experience.
